Hotel Overview

Rosewood Hotels & Resorts is a globally renowned luxury hotel group known for its refined hospitality, personalized service, and unique sense of place. Established in 1979, Rosewood operates ultra-luxury hotels, resorts, and residences in some of the worlds most stunning destinations.

Guests enjoy world-class amenities, including Michelin-star dining, holistic wellness at Asaya spas, curated cultural experiences, and unparalleled hospitality. Whether its a stay at Htel de Crillon in Paris, Rosewood London, or Las Ventanas al Paraso in Mexico, Rosewood delivers sophistication, exclusivity, and an unforgettable journey.
