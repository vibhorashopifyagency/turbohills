Paris, France

  • Capital - Paris
  • Currency - Euro
  • Language - franaise
    English is widely spoken in tourist areas.

Paris, known as the "City of Light" (La Ville Lumire), is the capital of France and one of the most romantic and iconic cities in the world. Known for its timeless architecture, world-class museums, charming streets, rich history, and exquisite cuisine, Paris is a must-visit destination for travelers from around the globe. Globally recognized as a fashion capital, Paris is the birthplace of haute couture and luxury brands like Chanel, Louis Vuitton, and Dior.
